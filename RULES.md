# Rules

## Product Rules

- Do not build a generic agent marketplace clone.
- Do not turn the MVP into a broad no-code platform.
- Do not support multiple agent categories in the first demo.
- Do not add features that weaken the core trust-and-governance story.

## Architecture Rules

- Do not couple domain logic directly to 0G SDK calls.
- Do not let `0G DA` become the primary application database.
- Do not make `0G Pay` a blocking dependency for the MVP.
- Do not put all app state onchain.
- Do not skip the manifest schema definition before UI growth.
- Do not design more contracts than the first flow requires.

## Trust and Safety Rules

- Do not use a black-box trust score.
- Do not claim verification unless there is a visible artifact or proof reference.
- Do not hide pause state, incident history, or ownership state.
- Do not treat ownership alone as sufficient trust.

## Delivery Rules

- Do not optimize for breadth over one complete working flow.
- Do not build monetization before the create-store-mint-list flow works.
- Do not overbuild compute integration before storage and minting are real.
- Do not ship unclear mocks as if they are real 0G integrations.
- Do not sacrifice demo reliability for extra features.
